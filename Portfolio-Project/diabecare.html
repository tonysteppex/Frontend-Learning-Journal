DiabeCare Mobile App

UX Design Engineer

2024

The goal was to create a user-friendly lifestyle companion app for individuals managing diabetes, addressing their day-to-day challenges with personalized tools.

View Prototype

Key Accomplishments

  • DiabeCare stands out for its focus on community-building alongside healthcare management, offering both practical and emotional support to users.
  • The result is an all-in-one diabetes management app that helps users track their health, receive medication reminders, and connect with others facing similar challenges.
  • The app provides seamless access to healthcare providers, fosters community support, and offers real-time advice from specialists.
